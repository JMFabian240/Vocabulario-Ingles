# TEMA 8: OBLIGACIONES Y REGLAS 

## EXPRESIONES
| EXPRESIONES | TRADUCCIÓN | EJEMPLO |
|---|---|---|
| You need to... | Necesitas... | You need to cut my hair. It is too long.  |
| I have to... | Tengo que... | I have to wear a uniform for work.  |
| Do you have to ...? | ¿Tienes que...? | Do you have to study for your exam?  |
| I don't have to... | No tengo que... | I don't have to stop smoking.  |
| I don't need to... | No necesito... | I don't need to buy a new car. This is OK  |
| I like to... | Me gusta... | I like to go to the movies on weekends.  |
| I don't like to... | No me gusta... | I don't like to eat vegetables.  |
| I really need to... | Realmente necesito... | I really need to pass my English exam.  |
| I'd like to... | Me gustaría... | I'd like to travel to USA with you.  |
| I want to... | Quiero... | I want to go to the movies tonight.  |
| I hate to | Odio... | I hate to wait people for a long time.  |
| I'm free on Saturdays | Estoy libre los sábados | Do you want to go the movies? Yes, I'm free on Saturdays.  |
