# TEMA 5: EVENTOS PASADOS 

## EXPRESIONES
| EXPRESIONES | TRADUCCIÓN | EJEMPLO |
|---|---|---|
| Did you go out...? | ¿Saliste...? | Did you go out last Friday night?  |
| Did you go to (city) ...? | ¿Fuiste a la ciudad? | Did you go to the city?  |
| What did you do? | ¿Qué hiciste? | What did you do last weekend?  |
| Where did you go...? | ¿A dónde fuiste...? | Where did you go yesterday?  |
| When did you go? | ¿Cuándo fuiste...? | When did you go to Europe?  |
| What time did you ...? | ¿A qué hora ...? | What time did you go to the movies last Saturday?  |
| How was...? | ¿Cómo fue/ estuvo...? | How was your weekend?  |
| When did you...? | ¿Cuándo tú...? | When did you read the book?  |
| Who did you go with? | ¿Con quién fuiste? | Who did you go on vacations with?  |
| Why did you...? | ¿Por qué...? | Why did you miss the school yesterday?  |
| How long did you...? | ¿Qué tanto tiempo .../ cuánto tardaste...? | How long did you stay in Asia?  |
| When I was a child... | Cuando era niño/a... | When I was a child, I lived with my grandma.  |
| My favorite ... was/were... | Mi... favorito/a (s) era/ eran | My favorite food was the pizza.  |
| My favorite teacher/cartoon/ toy was... | Mi maestro/ caricatura/juguete favorito/a era... | My favorite cartoon was Sponge Bob.  |
| Were you...? | ¿Tú eras/estabas...? | Were you at home yesterday?  |
| What was/were you... like? | ¿Cómo era/eras tú? | What were you like?  |
| When/ where were you born? | ¿Cuándo/dónde naciste? | A: When were you born? B: I was born on April 23rd.  |
| Who was your...? | ¿Quén era tu...? | Who was your favorite singer?  |
| There was/wasn't a party (at the beach). There were/ weren't many videogames at the mall. | Hubo/ No hubo una fiesta (en la playa). Había/ No había muchos video juegos en el centro comercial. | There was a party at the beach. There were many videogames at the mall.  |
| Was there a museum in the town? Yes, there was./ No, there wasn't. | ¿Había un museo en la ciudad? Sí, sí había. / No, no había. | A: Was there a museum in the town? B: Yes, there was.  |
| Were there any shopping centers? Yes, there were. / No, there weren't. | ¿Había centros comerciales? Sí, sí había. / No, no había. | A: Were there any shopping centers? B: No, there weren't.  |
