# TEMA 4: TIENDAS Y RESTAURANTES 

## EXPRESIONES 
| EXPRESIONES | TRADUCCIÓN | EJEMPLO |
|---|---|---|
| Would you like to ...? | ¿Le gustaría...? | A: Would you like to drink some water? B: Yes, please.  |
| I would like to order a ... | Me gustaría ordenar... | I would like to order a pizza.  |
| How much is the...? | ¿Cuánto es el/la...? | A: How much is the pizza? B: It's 200 pesos.  |
| How much are the...? | ¿Cuántos son las/los...? | A: How much are the books? B: They are 650 pesos.  |
| How much does it cost? | ¿Cuánto cuesta esto? | A: How much does it cost? B: It's 80 pesos.  |
| How much is it? | ¿Cuánto es? | A: Excuse, how much is it? B: It is 26 pesos.  |
| This one. | Éste. | A: Which dress do you like better? B: This one. It is beautiful.  |
| That one. | Aquél. | A: Which blouse do you prefer? B: That one. It is near to the window.  |
| The red one. | El rojo. | A: Which one do you like more? B: The red one.  |
| I'll take it. | Lo tomaré/lo llevaré | A: Do you like this blue scarf? B: Yes, I do. I'll take it.  |
| I'll take the red one. | Tomaré/llevaré el rojo. | A: Which hat do you prefer? B: I'll take the red one.  |
| Here you are. | Aquí tienes. | A: The shoes are 350 pesos. B: Here you are.  |
| May/Can I help you? | ¿Puedo ayudarte? | A: Hello, may I help you? B: Yes, please.  |
| Could/Can I try it on? | ¿Me lo puedo probar? | A: Here you have the t-shirt. B: Can I try it on?  |
| What size are you? | ¿Qué talla eres? | A: What size are you? B: I am medium size.  |
| Do you have/ can I have larger/ smaller size? | ¿Tiene/me puede traer una talla más grande/ pequeña? | A: Can I have a larger size? B: Sure.  |
| Just looking | Solo estoy mirando | A: May I help you? B: I'm just looking.  |
| Anything else? | ¿Algo más? | A: Do you need anything else? B: No, I don't. Thank you.  |
| Pay by credit card/ debit card/check/ in cash | Pagar con tarjeta de crédito/ tarjeta de débito/con cheque/ en efectivo | I'll pay in cash.  |
| I'll buy/pay/ have | Yo compraré/ pagaré/ llevaré | I'll buy a t-shirt.  |
| Can I use the restroom? | ¿Puedo usar el baño? | A: Can I use the restroom? B: Of course.  |
| Can I have glass of water, please? | ¿Me podría traer un vaso de agua? Por favor. | A: Can I have a glass of water, please? B: Sure.  |
| There are few people in the restaurant. | Hay pocas personas en el restaurante. | There are few poeple in the restaurant.  |
| How many...? | ¿Cuántos/cuántas? | A: How many hamburgers do you want? B: Two, please.  |
