# TEMA 7: FUTURO PARA PLANES Y PROYECTOS 

## EXPRESIONES
| EXPRESIONES | TRADUCCIÓN | EJEMPLO |
|---|---|---|
| Are you going to...? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not. Yes, I'm going to... No, I'm not going to... | ¿Vas a ...? Sí, sí lo haré. / No, no lo haré. Sí, yo voy a ... No, yo no voy a ... | Are you going to go to the cinema? Yes, I am.  |
| What are you going to...? I am going to... | ¿Qué vas a ...? Yo voy a... | What are you going to eat? I am going to watch TV.  |
| Where are you going to...? | ¿A dónde vas a ...? | Where are you going to study?  |
| When/How/Who's going to...? | ¿Cuándo/Cómo/ Quién va a...? | When are you going to visit your mother?  |
| What is he/she going to do next vacation? | ¿Qué hará ella/él las próximas vacaciones? | What is he going to do next vacation?  |
| Who's going to go...? | ¿Quién irá...? | Who's going to go to the cinema?  |
| Who are you going to go with? | ¿Con quién irás? | A: Who are you going to go with? B: I'm going to go with my friends.  |
| Why are you going to...? | ¿Por qué vas a...? | Why are going to the supermarket?  |
| Why is she/he going to...? | ¿Por qué él/ella va a...? | Why is she going to your house?  |
| How long are you going to...? | ¿Por cuánto tiempo vas a...? | How long are you going to study English?  |
| How long is he/she going to...? | ¿Por cuánto tiempo él/ella va a...? | How long is she going to do exercise?  |
| She/ He is going to ... | Él/ella va... | He's going to read.  |
| What are your vacation plans for next week? | ¿Cuáles son tus planes para las vacaciones de la siguiente semana? | What are your vacation plans for next week?  |
